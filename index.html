ESP8266 Firmware for Octoblu

With this Firmware your ESP8266 can communicate with Octoblu

Features

  • mqtt connection to octoblu
  • controlling WS2812 RGB LEDs
  • automatic updating (in progress)

Requirements

  • nodemcu firmware installed (> 0.9.5)
  • ESPlorer or alternate uploader
